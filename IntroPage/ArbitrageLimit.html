You will be trading one stock in a fictitious market on behalf of investors into your fund. Please read and understand the following parameters:

You have a set amount of funds with which to trade
Your investors will have a loss tolerance for losses of some percent You know with 100% certainty that the true value of the stock will be reached

You will be presented with 5 rounds of trading, in which the last round the stock reaches it's true value. You may buy as many units of the stock as you have funds in that time period. You may sell any amount of the stock at the start of a time period.

Your investors will pull their funding if your funds at the start of the period are below your initial funds multiplied by the loss tolerance of investors.

If you are liquidated by investors you will earn 0. Otherwise you will earn your profit in the last time period divided by 20, rounded up to nearest dollar.
